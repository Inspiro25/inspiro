About The Event

Inspiro'25 is a premier IT festival that brings together tech enthusiasts, innovators, and creative minds under one vibrant platform. This annual event celebrates the spirit of innovation, fostering a culture of collaboration and excellence in the field of information technology. With a diverse range of activities such as coding challenges, hackathons, workshops, tech talks, and cultural extravaganzas, Inspiro'25 is designed to inspire participants to explore the limitless possibilities of technology. The event serves as a melting pot for ideas, offering opportunities to learn, network, and showcase talents while staying ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape. Join us at Inspiro'25 to ignite your imagination, push boundaries, and be a part of the future of IT!

Event venue location info

Don Bosco Arts and Science College, Angadikadavu

Don Bosco Arts & Science College had a humble beginning in 1994 as a Parallel College, offering pre-degree, undergraduate and postgraduate courses of Calicut University for about 600 students, with Fr. Thomas Poovelickal SDB as Rector and Manager, and Bro. Shaji Kalavanal SDB as Principal.
